Food Part 2 (A0-A1)

Look at these words and expressions.
Посмотри на эти слова и выражения.

  • Eat – есть, кушать
  • Drink – пить
  • Have breakfast – завтракать
  • Have lunch – обедать
  • Have dinner – ужинать

Read the text.
Прочитайте текст.

Hello! My name is Satya. I am a student. In the morning I eat cake and drink juice. I have lunch at school. I eat bread, cheese and fruit and drink tea. For dinner, I eat soup with bread. I like pizza but I don't eat it every day. In the evening I drink milk.

Try to guess the meaning of the words. Check yourself.
Угадайте значения слов. Проверьте себя.
