Hare Krishna! Please, accept our humble obeisances! All glories to Srila Prabhupada!

Welcome to our website! It was created to help devotees who want to learn the English language to do it using materials that help maintain Krishna Consciousness.

Learn language by watching entertaining videos, listening to fascinating stories from Srimad Bhagavatam and other scriptures about Krishna, His friends and Lord's Incarnations. All of them contain wisdom of humankind.

Grammar rules won't be a problem. They are explained in the simplest way possible and provided with examples, schemes, tables and colourful pictures. After the rule there are exercises, tests and games.

You get access to a range of exercises with keys. For your convinience all of them are divided into groups according to their level. Apart from that, the exercises are grouped by the activity (Listening, Reading, Grammar, etc.) You also can find games to develop different aspects of your language.

The modern design of the website will make your experience still more enjoyable. We did our best to make the website as convinient as possible. It's not perfect but it's still a humble attempt on our part.

If you know your level of English, just choose the necessery level in the menu at the beginning of the page. If not, you will find a link to a quality test to find out your level in the menu.

If you like the website and find it helpful, please, take a few minutes to say thanks. We really appreciate your feedback!

Good luck! Hope, you enjoy the experience!
