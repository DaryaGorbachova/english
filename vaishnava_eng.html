Welcome to our website! It was created to help devotees who want to learn the English language to do it using Krishna Consciousness materials.

Learn language by watching entertaining videos, listening to fascinating stories from Srimad Bhagavatam and other scriptures about Krishna, His friends, Lord's Incarnations. All of them contain wisdom of humankind.

Grammar rules won't be a problem. They are explained in the simplest way possible and provided with examples, schemes, tables and colourful pictures.

You get access to a range of exercises with keys. For your convinience all of them are divided into groups according to their level. Apart from that, the exercises are grouped by the activity (Listening, Reading, Grammar, etc.)

The modern design will make your experience on the website still more enjoyable. We did our best to make the website as convinient as possible. It's not perfect but it's still a humble attempt on our part.

If you know your level of English, just choose the necessery level in the menu at the beginning of the page. If not, you will find a link to a quality test in the menu.

Good luck! Hope, you enjoy the experience!
