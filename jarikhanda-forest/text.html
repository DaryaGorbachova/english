Jarikhanda Forest (A0-A1)

Read the words.

Прочитайте слова.

  • Alone – в одиночку
  • Together – вместе
  • Agree – согласиться
  • Travel – путешествовать
  • Decide – решить
  • Stand up – встать
  • Touch – дотронуться
  • Parrot – попугай
  • Monkey – обезьяна
  • Elephant – слон
  • Bird – птица
  • Road – дорога
  • Go deeper – идти вглубь
  • At once – сразу же
  • Loudly – громко
  • No longer – больше не
  • Enemy – враг
  • Devotee – преданный
  • Scared / Afraid – напуганный
  • Disappointed – расстроенный

Read the text.

Прочитайте текст.

Chaitanya Mahaprabhu wants to go to Vrindavana. He wants to go alone but agrees to take Balabhadra Bhattacharya with Him. At night they leave Jagannatha Puri. In the morning everyone is disappointed when they can’t find Lord Chaitanya.

Lord Chaitanya decides to travel through Jarikhanda forest. It is a dangerous place. There are a lot of tigers, lions, parrots, monkeys and elephants. But He is not afraid. He sings Hare Krishna mantra. But Balabhadra Bhattacharya is scared. He sees that Lord Chaitanya goes very close to a tiger on the road. Lord touches it and says, “Chant the names of Krishna!”. The tiger at once stands up and begins to sing “Krishna! Krishna!”

Later Lord Chaitanya sees elephants near the river. Again He says, “Chant the names of Krishna!”. The elephants also begin to sing “Krishna! Krishna!”

Lord Chaitanya goes deeper into the jungle. He sings so loudly that different animals come to Him. Tigers, deer, elephants, monkeys, birds and snakes sing together the Holy Name. They are no longer enemies. Even the trees sing too.

Lord Chaitanya makes everyone Krishna’s devotees.

Use the verb in Present Simple. You can read the rule here.

Употребите глагол в настоящем простом времени. Правило здесь.

  1. He (sing).
  2. They (sing).
  3. He (travel).
  4. They (travel).
  5. He (see).
  6. They (see).
  7. Lord Chaitanya (decide) to travel alone.
  8. The tiger (stand up) and (sing).
  9. Balabhadra Bhattacharya (go) with Chaitanya.
  10. They (decide) to go through Jarikhanda forest.
  11. Balabhadra Bhattacharya (cook) for Lord Chaitanya.
  12. They (leave) the city and (go) into the jungle.
  13. Lord (touch) the tiger.
  14. Animals (begin) to sing Krishna’s names.
  15. Lord (agree) to take Balabhadra Bhattacharya with Him.
